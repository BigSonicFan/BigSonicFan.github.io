I got into video games when I was younger thanks to my sister. I don't have a specific favorite genre of video games because I honestly love a variety of them. My most favorites would be the games from the franchises I grew up with.

Sewing Crafts
One of the hobbies I wanted to get into since I was younger. It was until 2017 where I start sewing things. Honestly, it was because I wanted to make my very own plushies, also known as stuffed animals. I have made a few plushies with my sewing skills for 2 years. While some use a sewing machine to sew things, I do hand-stitching, which adds a little more love into creations in my opinion.
